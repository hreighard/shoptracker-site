Who We Are

A New Team For A New Series

We are the Formula Electric design team at Missouri University of Science and Technology in Rolla, Missouri. Participating in the Formula SAE Electric series of racing, we design, build, and race a scale formula-style car each year. While Formula SAE has been around for a long time, the electric series has only recently come to the U.S. In 2012 a group of students got together to form an entirely new team to race in the new series, and Formula Electric was born.
